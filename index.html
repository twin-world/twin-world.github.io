About

TwinWorld brings together researchers and practitioners working at the intersection of computer vision, robotics, geospatial AI, autonomous systems, and construction technology. The workshop aims to advance scalable digital twins through multimodal 3D and 4D scene understanding, semantic modeling, neural representations, and benchmark-driven evaluation.
